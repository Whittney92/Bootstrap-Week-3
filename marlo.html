Hey Guys, my name is Marlo and I am a rescue dog. My mom rescued me from a neighbor next door that kept me chained up to fence outside with little to no food and water. My mom rescued me at 1yrs old, I was under weight and dirty. I first laid eyes on my mom when I was just a few months old we instantly had a connection.
I remember the day my mom resuced me, I was SO HAPPY, That was the first day I received a bath, I was terrified! She then put me in the car and took me to my new home! My anxiety was through the WROOF... sorry, I meant roof.
